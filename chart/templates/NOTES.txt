To get started with kslack:

1. Check the deployment status:
   kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "kslack.name" . }}

2. View the logs:
   kubectl logs -f -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "kslack.name" . }}

3. Verify the bot is connected:
   Look for "Connecting to Slack via Socket Mode" in the logs

4. In Slack, invite the bot to a channel:
   /invite @kagent-bot

5. Test the bot with a message:
   @kagent-bot hello

6. List available agents:
   /agents

7. Switch to a specific agent:
   /agent-switch kagent/k8s-agent
